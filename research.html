Research

I'm interested in reasoning reliability, interpretability, and decision transparency in LLM and multi-agent systems. The through-line: AI that can be held accountable for its outputs.

Publications

Counterfactual Self-Questioning for LLM Reasoning (CSQ)
Mandar Narendra Parab  ·  Preprint, 2025  ·  arxiv:2601.00885
A framework where a single LLM generates counterfactual critiques of its own reasoning as a structured policy optimization signal — eliminating the need for external reward models or multiple agents. Integrates directly with GRPO. Evaluated on GSM8K, MATH, and Minerva-style tasks: +6.7 to +12.4 points over chain-of-thought and +3.1 to +5.8 points over strong baselines.
GRPO Reasoning GSM8K Policy Optimization Preprint
